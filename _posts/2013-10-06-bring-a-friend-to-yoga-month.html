Fall is filled with the beauty of creation, and is a great time for changing and breaking habits.As the weather chills the air, and we turn on our heaters, you may start to notice your skin becomes drier, and elimination may be slower with your change in diet. Making this season comfortable, bright, and healthy can be accomplished in 3 steps...

  1. Cleanse your body for the fall/winter harvest.
  2. Don’t be sedimentary. Exercise will keep the fire in your belly for healthy digestion and elimination.
  3. Allow extra time for skin care. Doing a dry scrub in the morning (or night), before you shower, will exfoliate your skin. After your shower (or isshnan) apply coconut oil (or almond oil) to your skin, starting with your feet and working your way up your body, lastly ending at your heart center.


Breaking habits and making change starts with a goal. I suggest doing a meditation everyday for 40 days, if possible at the same time everyday. Make a commitment to yourself to break unwanted habits with this ritual. Don’t overwhelm yourself with the little things. As you meditate you’ll notice how stress, and hard limits start to roll off you like water on oil. Enjoy this time of year by accepting change, and letting go.
